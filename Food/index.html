Food Details

Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and meat (chicken, lamb, or beef).

Parotta

Parotta is a popular South Indian layered flatbread made with all-purpose flour. It is usually served with curry or side dishes.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern street food made with thinly sliced meat (often lamb or chicken) that is stacked in a cone shape and cooked on a vertical rotisserie.

Grill Chicken

Grill chicken is a delicious dish where chicken is marinated and grilled to perfection, resulting in a smoky and flavorful taste.
